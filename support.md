# Support

**Together in Faith**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the app work?
Together in Faith helps you and your partner build a daily devotional habit. Each day, both partners mark their devotional as complete. The app tracks your streak, displays an inspiring Bible verse, and celebrates milestones along the way.

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the internet. Your names, devotional history, milestones, and reflections stay on your phone.

### How do I set up daily reminders?
Go to the **Settings** tab and toggle on **Daily Reminder**. The app will ask for notification permission, then you can choose your preferred reminder time.

### Can I change the names displayed in the app?
Yes. Go to the **Settings** tab and update "My Name" and "Partner's Name" to whatever you like.

### What are milestones?
Milestones celebrate your devotional consistency as a couple. You'll unlock milestones at 7, 14, 30, 60, 90, 180, and 365 days of shared devotionals. Each milestone includes an inspirational Bible verse.

### What are weekly reflections?
Weekly reflections give you and your partner a space to write about your spiritual journey together. A new prompt appears each week, and both partners can share their thoughts.

### How do I reset my progress?
Go to **Settings** and tap **Reset Couple Streak**. This clears your devotional history and milestones but keeps your settings and weekly reflections.

### I lost my data after reinstalling the app
Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will permanently delete your data. Unfortunately there is no way to recover it after reinstallation.

### The app isn't sending notifications
1. Make sure Daily Reminder is toggled on in the Settings tab
2. Go to your device's **Settings > Notifications > Together in Faith** and ensure notifications are allowed
3. Check that Do Not Disturb or Focus modes are not blocking notifications
