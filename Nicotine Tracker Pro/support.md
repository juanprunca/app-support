# Support

**Nicotine Tracker Pro**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the app work?
Nicotine Tracker Pro lets you log each nicotine intake throughout the day. You can track different nicotine types (vape, cigarettes, patches, gum, lozenges, pouches), set daily goals, and view your consumption history over time.

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the internet. Your intake entries, goals, and settings stay on your phone.

### Why do I need to verify my age?
Nicotine is an addictive substance restricted to adults. The app requires you to confirm you are 18 or older before use. This is a one-time verification on first launch.

### How do I log a nicotine intake?
Tap the **+** button on the Home screen. Select your nicotine type, choose or enter the nicotine strength (in mg), set the quantity, and optionally add notes. Tap Save to log the entry.

### What nicotine types are supported?
The app supports Vape, Cigarette, Patch, Gum, Lozenge, and Pouch. You can set your default type in Settings.

### How do I set a daily goal?
Go to the **Goals** tab, enable the daily goal toggle, and set your target daily nicotine intake in milligrams. The Home screen will show your progress against this goal.

### How do I view my history?
Tap the **History** tab to see your nicotine consumption over time. You can switch between Week, Month, and Year views to see trends in your intake.

### How do I set up daily reminders?
Go to the **Settings** tab and toggle on **Daily Reminders**. The app will ask for notification permission, then you can choose your preferred reminder time.

### How do I export my data?
Go to **Settings** and tap **Export Data**. This creates a JSON file containing all your entries, goals, and settings that you can save or share.

### I lost my data after reinstalling the app
Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will permanently delete your data. We recommend using the Export Data feature in Settings to back up your data periodically.

### The app isn't sending notifications
1. Make sure Daily Reminders is toggled on in the Settings tab
2. Go to your device's **Settings > Notifications > Nicotine Tracker Pro** and ensure notifications are allowed
3. Check that Do Not Disturb or Focus modes are not blocking notifications
