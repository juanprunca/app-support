# Privacy Policy

**Nicotine Tracker Pro**
**Effective Date: March 1, 2026**

## Overview

Nicotine Tracker Pro is a personal nicotine intake tracking app designed to help you monitor and manage your nicotine consumption. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

## Data Collection

**Nicotine Tracker Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

All information you enter into the app — including your nicotine intake entries, goals, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data ever leaves your device.

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do **not** collect personal information
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking tools
- We do **not** share data with third parties
- We do **not** use advertising SDKs
- We do **not** require an account or login
- We do **not** transmit any data over the internet

## Local Data Storage

The app uses the following local storage mechanisms on your device:

- **UserDefaults** — for saving your app settings and preferences
- **Local file storage** — for saving nicotine intake entries and goals in your app's private Documents directory

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Notifications

If you choose to enable daily reminders, the app uses Apple's local notification system. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

## Age Restriction

Nicotine Tracker Pro requires users to verify they are 18 years of age or older before using the app. The date of birth entered for age verification is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Children's Privacy

Nicotine Tracker Pro is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. The app includes an age verification gate and does not knowingly collect any information from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
