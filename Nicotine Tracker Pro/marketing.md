# Nicotine Tracker Pro

**Take control of your nicotine intake — one log at a time.**

## About

Nicotine Tracker Pro is a clean, private, and easy-to-use app that helps you monitor your daily nicotine consumption. Whether you're trying to cut back, set limits, or simply understand your habits, Nicotine Tracker Pro gives you the tools to stay informed and in control.

## Features

### Quick Intake Logging
Log your nicotine intake in seconds. Select your nicotine type, choose from common strength presets or enter a custom value, and save. It's that simple.

### Multiple Nicotine Types
Track vaping, cigarettes, patches, gum, lozenges, and pouches — all in one place. Set your default type for even faster logging.

### Daily Goals
Set a daily nicotine limit and watch your progress throughout the day. A clear progress bar on the Home screen keeps you motivated and aware.

### Consumption History
View your intake trends over the past week, month, or year with easy-to-read charts. Identify patterns and track your progress over time.

### Daily Reminders
Set a gentle daily reminder so you never forget to log your intake.

### Export Your Data
Export all your entries, goals, and settings as a JSON file for your records or to share with a healthcare provider.

### Privacy First
All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud storage, no tracking. Your health data is yours alone.
