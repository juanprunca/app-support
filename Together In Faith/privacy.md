# Privacy Policy

**Together in Faith**
**Effective Date: March 1, 2026**

## Overview

Together in Faith is a daily couple devotional app designed to help partners grow in their faith journey together. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

## Data Collection

**Together in Faith does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

All information you enter into the app — including your names, devotional history, milestones, and weekly reflections — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data ever leaves your device.

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do **not** collect personal information
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking tools
- We do **not** share data with third parties
- We do **not** use advertising SDKs
- We do **not** require an account or login
- We do **not** transmit any data over the internet

## Local Data Storage

The app uses the following local storage mechanisms on your device:

- **UserDefaults** — for saving your app settings and preferences
- **Local file storage** — for saving devotional entries, milestones, and reflections in your app's private Documents directory

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Notifications

If you choose to enable daily reminders, the app uses Apple's local notification system. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Together in Faith does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect information from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
