# Together in Faith

**Grow closer to God and each other — one day at a time.**

## About

Together in Faith is a beautifully simple devotional app built for couples who want to strengthen their spiritual bond. Track your daily devotionals side by side, reflect on your journey together, and celebrate every milestone along the way.

## Features

### Daily Devotional Tracking
Mark your devotional as complete each day and see your partner's progress too. When both of you complete your devotional, the app celebrates with you.

### Couple Streak
Build a streak of consecutive days where both partners complete their devotional. Watch your commitment grow day after day.

### Daily Bible Verses
Receive a new inspirational Bible verse every day to guide your faith journey together.

### Milestone Celebrations
Unlock meaningful milestones at 7, 14, 30, 60, 90, 180, and 365 days. Each milestone comes with an inspiring verse to mark the occasion.

### Weekly Reflections
Share your thoughts and feelings about your spiritual journey with your partner through guided weekly reflection prompts.

### Calendar History
View your devotional history at a glance with a beautiful calendar showing your individual and shared progress.

### Daily Reminders
Set a gentle daily reminder so you never miss your devotional time together.

### Privacy First
All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud storage, no tracking. Your faith journey is yours alone.
